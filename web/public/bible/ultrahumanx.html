Overview

The UltrahumanX Store is an in-app destination offering a variety of Ultrahuman products.

  • Who can benefit? Users with an active UltrahumanX subscription unlock special offers and exclusive discounts on product purchases within this store.
  • How to access? Go to Profile > UltrahumanX Store on the Ultrahuman app.

App experience

With UltrahumanX

  • The app icon for the profile tab will be visible with an “X” for all UltrahumanX users.
  • It will also show the validity of their subscription in Profile > Settings.
  • UltrahumanX users see a different version of the pricing page which will show deals/offers.

Without UltrahumanX

  • The app icon for profile tab does not have a visible “X” mark.
  • Clicking the UltrahumanX Store asks users to “Become a member”.
  • Non UltrahumanX users will not have any deals/offers on UltrahumanX products. The UltrahumanX membership will be included on the checkout page of the store (Billed 199/month).
