Ring AIR

Overview

The Ultrahuman Ring AIR is 11x lighter and significantly sleeker than most smartwatches out on the market today, with the smallest Ultrahuman Ring AIR being as light as 2.4 grams.

It is specifically crafted to withstand demanding usage conditions. Constructed with a Titanium body and coated in Tungsten carbide, renowned for its exceptional hardness, this ring offers remarkable durability.

The Ultrahuman Ring AIR is water resistant up to 100m. You can wear it while swimming, showering, and even scuba diving. We do not recommend keeping the ring submerged underwater for more than 30 minutes. Please note this water resistance is higher than the max Ingress protection (IPX9) rating that any consumer electronic product could be rated.

Colours

The Ring AIR is available in 6 colours: Matte Grey, Aster Black, Space Silver, Bionic Gold, Raw Titanium and Brushed Rose Gold.

More Information

The Ring Air empowers users with deep insights derived from metrics such as movement data, HRV, Heart Rate and Sleep Data. These metrics, when combined with our existing ecosystem of products such as Ultrahuman M1 can provide proactive correlations to take your metabolic health to the next level.

The Ring is designed to help you understand the factors that affect your metabolism. With the ring you can:

  1. Understand your activity patterns throughout the day with the Movement Index based on metabolic equivalents (METs). It counts calories, steps etc.
  2. Decode your sleep health with the Sleep Index and understand how your sleep quality affects your metabolism.
  3. Comprehend body metrics like stress using the Body Index based on your heart rate, HRV and body temperature.

Product Overview

The Ring is our next big step towards decoding metabolic health and bringing personalized data-driven insights into everyone's reach.

The Thought & Design behind the ring

Following the Ultrahuman M1, we sought to create a device that could track a more detailed set of metabolic biomarkers while prioritizing convenience and subtlety.

The result is the Ultrahuman Ring, a wearable designed to sit comfortably and discreetly on your finger.

  • Effortless Tracking: The Ring continuously monitors your biosignals in the background, making it a truly "wear-and-forget" experience.
  • Intentionally Simple: We deliberately avoided constant notifications, screens, and complex interfaces. The Ring provides a clean, non-intrusive experience, allowing you to access your health data on your own terms through the accompanying app.

What does the ring do?

The Ultrahuman Ring is specifically designed to help you gain a deep understanding of your metabolic health. It achieves this by continuously tracking and analyzing key factors that influence your metabolism:

  • Sleep Quality
  • Movement (Activity)
  • Body Metrics: Heart Rate Variability (HRV), Heart Rate, and Body Temperature

Biomarkers for you

To make it easy for you to decode your metabolic health, we give you three different scores for each aspect of your health. These scores help you understand how to optimize your sleep, activity and recovery in a truly personal way.

The Movement Index

The Movement Index helps you understand your daily activity patterns using a metric called METs (Metabolic Equivalents).

What are METs?

  • 1 MET: The energy you burn while completely at rest (your resting metabolic rate).
  • Example: Brisk walking is typically around 4 METs, meaning you are expending four times the energy compared to when you are resting.

How it helps you:

  1. Understand your current activity levels.
  2. Nudge you with personalized and recommended activities to help you improve your score and encourage you to keep moving throughout the day.

The Sleep Index

The Sleep Index allows you to decode your sleep quality and see a breakdown of your sleep stages throughout the night. Poor sleep quality affects your metabolic health and poor metabolic health affects your sleep quality creating a feedback loop. The Ultrahuman ring lets you derive correlations between how your sleep stages like REM/NREM affect your metabolism and vice-versa, thus becoming your key to unlock the mysteries of a good night sleep and a well rested heart.

The Recovery Index / Dynamic Recovery

  • The Ring AIR's Dynamic Recovery is an aggregate measure of five parameters determining your body's preparedness for the next day and changes throughout the day. It is a percentage measure from 0-100, a higher (85 or above) number indicating better recovery.
  • The Dynamic Recovery has six factors which include sleep index, skin temperature, stress rhythm score, resting heart rate, HRV form and last night HRV.
  • Dynamic Recovery is dynamic and can be improved throughout the day by regulating stress, maintaining good sleep and sleep hygiene, proper exercise routine, eating balanced and timely meals, hydrating well and including cold therapy and active recovery.

What is the ring made of?

The Ultrahuman is designed to be tough on the outside and comfortable on the inside. The ring is crafted using:

  • A Titanium alloy billet for the body.
  • A super durable Tungsten Carbide coating which is 5 times harder than Tool Steel making it remarkably scratch-resistant and ready to take on everyday wear and tear.
  • Medical grade transparent material on the insides that have been tested safe to be used even for implanting inside the human body.
  • The ring is designed hypoallergenic and can be worn for multiple days without causing any harm to the skin.

The inside shape of the ring has multiple sections for each sensor protruding towards your finger, separated by a streamlined design with filleted edges to enable the sensors to take accurate readings while still keeping the Ultrahuman Ring comfortable enough to wear for weeks on end.

Other Characteristics

Battery life: The battery ideally lasts from 4-6 days depending upon the usage. However, we are continuously working on optimizing the battery life of the Ultrahuman Ring with regular firmware updates to provide you with a longer-lasting experience. The battery life of 3.5 to 4 days is influenced by several factors, including ambient temperature, frequency of use, and individual usage patterns. When we advertise up to 6 days of battery life, we consider optimal conditions where the ring is worn continuously and the ambient temperature is ideal. We understand your expectations and are continuously working to optimise the ring's performance under various conditions.

Charge time: It takes around 180 minutes to fully charge the battery.

Syncing: We recommend syncing your ring at least once every 24 hours to ensure all your biosignals are safely transferred to the app and to prevent any potential loss of data.

Compatibility: The ring is compatible with Android devices running on Android 6 and above. On iOS, it is 15 and above.

Materials: The Ultrahuman ring is made of an incredibly strong Titanium body and coated with a scratch-resistant Tungsten Carbide layer. The ring is hypoallergenic, with the insides made of medical-grade transparent materials - tough on the outside, comfortable on the inside.

Content ecosystem: The Ultrahuman Ring plugs into Ultrahuman's premium content created with world-class coaches, psychologists and experts. This means we can suggest one of our workout programs that would be just perfect for you to improve your Movement Index, or something from our Sleep Series to better your Sleep Index. We can suggest one of our meditation programs based on your stress levels, Body Index and so on. We don't just give you metrics, we also provide you with world-class tools to improve your health holistically.

Bluetooth sync: The ring connects to your device via Bluetooth and transfers your biosignals to the Ultrahuman App when you open the app and sync the ring. We recommend syncing your ring at least once every 24 hours to ensure all biosignals are safely transferred and to prevent potential data loss. On iOS, the ring syncs automatically as soon as you open the Ultrahuman App. On Android, open the Ultrahuman App and manually initiate a sync to transfer the data.

Charging: The ring charges with the custom charger included in the box. It takes approx 180 minutes to charge. You can expect around 3.5-4 days of battery life on a fully charged ring.

Pricing: You can visit the Ultrahuman Ring page to see up-to-date pricing.

Support: You will also get access to a team of certified performance coaches who can help you interpret your data better and give recommendations.

The Ultra-Verse (Ultrahuman Ecosystem)

The Ultrahuman Ring seamlessly integrates with existing products like the Ultrahuman M1, creating a powerful, cohesive view of your metabolic health. By combining the Ring's sleep, movement, and body data with the M1's glucose data, you unlock deep correlations and predictive, actionable insights.

Here is how this convergence works:

  1. Movement & Glucose Data: Predictive Spikes

    The power: You receive predictive insights based on your historical glucose patterns.

    Example: If eating pizza previously caused a high glucose spike, the next time you log it, the system will recommend personalized movement goals (for example, a brisk walk) to help mitigate or "cut" the expected spike before it happens.

  2. Sleep & Glucose Data: Real-Time Recovery Impact

    The power: See in real-time how your sleep quality affects your physiological recovery (HRV) and how both variables influence glucose regulation.

    Example: If your historical data shows that poor sleep leads to higher-than-usual glucose spikes for the same meal, the Ring and M1 work together:

    • The Ring signals poor sleep/recovery.
    • The M1 confirms the higher glucose spike.
    • Cohesive Insight: You get a complete picture showing that lack of recovery is making you less insulin sensitive.
  3. HRV & Glucose Data: Stress and Metabolism

    The power: Get real-time updates on your stress levels and recovery based on your Heart Rate Variability (HRV). Paired with glucose data, you can understand the deeper correlations between emotional/physiological stress and stress-induced glucose spikes.

Trade-In Process (Step-by-Step)

Purchase

  • The user places an order for the Ultrahuman Ring AIR at the full retail price.
  • During checkout, the user selects the trade-in option.
  • Trade-in product details (variant, size, model, etc.) are recorded on our end.

Delivery of New Device

  • We ship the Ultrahuman Ring AIR to the user as a standard order.
  • No trade-in ring needs to be shipped by the user at this stage.

Initiating the Return of the Old Device

  • Once the user receives the Ultrahuman Ring AIR, they contact Ultrahuman Support.
  • The support team assists in generating an RTO (Return to Origin) request.
  • The user will send their trade-in device to our nearest warehouse.

Verification

  • After we receive the device, the team inspects it.
  • If the device satisfies the trade-in acceptance criteria, it is approved.

Refund of Trade-In Credit

  • Once approved, the trade-in credit refund is initiated to the user's original payment method.
  • The refund may take 7-10 business days to reflect in their bank account.

How to Request an RTO in OMS

  1. Go to Ring Orders in the Ops dashboard.
  2. Select the order for which you want to initiate the RTO.
  3. Click Initiate RTO.
  4. Choose Trade-In from the reason dropdown.
  5. Fill in the necessary fields (device type, variant, user remarks, etc.).
  6. Submit the request.

Size Protection

If your ring size changes within 1 year of purchase, we offer an exchange for your RARE Ring to the nearest size, provided you meet the eligibility criteria.

Eligibility Criteria

  • It is only applicable for original owner who has purchased from an authorised retailer / outlet. Proof of purchase for the RARE Ring is required to initiate the size protection claim.
  • If the ring changes hands and/or is resold to someone else, the size protection will not be applicable if it has already been availed on that ring before once, despite the customer having the invoice.
  • Ultrahuman team will send a sizing kit to the customer to confirm the updated Ring size before proceeding with the replacement.
  • Once the new size is confirmed, Ultrahuman will ship an AIR Ring (in the revised size) for temporary use, ensuring the customer can continue tracking data while their Rare Ring is being under process.
  • The policy does not cover damages or technical issues unrelated to size changes.

Process Overview

Customer Actions:

  1. Initiate Request: Contact Ultrahuman support through the designated portal or helpline.
  2. Provide Details:
    • Share the original invoice and Ring ID.
    • Address details to ship the sizing kit.
    • Confirm new size via the sizing kit.
  3. Prepare for Shipping (only after revised size is approved): Pack the Ring securely following instructions provided by Ultrahuman.

Ultrahuman Actions:

  1. Verify details: check that the invoice is original, and Ring ID and see if it is marked to Rare Ring and given customer details.
  2. Ship sizing kit to the customer: Customer must try the latest size and confirm.
  3. Verification of size change: Confirm the revised size submitted by the customer.
  4. Arrange return shipping: Provide a prepaid shipping label and explain shipping instructions.
    1. To be shipped to our warehouse in that region (US, UK, EU and if not then UAE Office) and the authenticity to be checked by checking the Hallmark ID and if it matches our system then purity tested.
  5. Inspection:
    1. Authenticate the metal of the returned Ring - our warehouse team to check the Hallmark ID.
    2. Test for damages beyond normal wear and tear - UH team to do that.
    3. Bring it back to India.
  6. Approve replacement: Ensure the request aligns with the eligibility criteria.
  7. Replacement shipping: Dispatch a replacement Ring in the customer's updated size along with a thank you card for their patience.

Timeline

  • Shipping of sizing kit: 5-7 days.
  • Testing of the revised size by customer: 3-4 days.
  • Verification of size change evidence: 3-5 days.
  • Return shipping to Ultrahuman: 5-7 days.
  • Ring inspection and processing: 3-5 days.
  • Replacement shipping to customer: 5-7 days.
